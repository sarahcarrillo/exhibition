For this project we had to do a total of two projects where the first on we had the theme of coding the past where we picked someone that we knew had an interesting life growing up. The project also included making a collage of different images representing our persons life and also writing an essay on one topic they mention in the interview. The second project we did was a timely soulution based on the time era of the industrial revolution.
